Lux

Bringing student ideas to life
outside of the classroom.

Using digital multimedia
to impact the community

Lux is a community dedicated to promoting
education and topics such as healthcare and
technology through the use of digital mult-
imedia. This includes digital art, photography,
videography, music production, and much
more.

An inter-university club

In addition to being a space to hang out, ask and answer questions, and talk about multimedia,
we are also an inter-university club (however all others are all welcome!) that works on
projects that aim to positively impact the greater community!
